"How much do we belong to the country of our first passport and how much do we belong to the 'other countries' that allow us to enter?"

Quote by Orhan Pamuk

In the globe above, you have seen the visualization of the population at each coordinate point on Earth. While many of us live in one location, we might not consider that as our home. This project intends to collect data on what you consider your home. To the question 'where is home?' there are some who might give a single-word answer naming a city or town, but other might have a list of places that they want to share. Whether you are the latter or the former person, you will have the opportunity to enter up to three locations that you consider your home. Click the button below to proceed with the questionnaire.
